Rich text editor (word-processor style)
  1. On your computer, run: python3 edit_server.py in this project folder (ask for help once if needed).
  2. In the browser, open http://127.0.0.1:8766/ — only works on the same computer.
  3. Choose a property in the dropdown, then scroll to “Story & broker note” right below.
  4. Click in the big white box. Use the gray toolbar above it: B bold, I italic, lists, and link. The preview further down updates after you pause typing.
  5. Click Save at the bottom when finished.
